Pablo o Pombo is an adventure game developed over two weeks for the PET Jam game jam held by PET-UFRGS. The game features ground and flying mechanics inspired by Minecraft's Elytra.

## Controls

**On Ground:**
- WASD - Move
- SPACE - Jump

**Flying:**
- SPACE - Begin flying after jump / Flap wings to gain height
- Similar mechanics to Minecraft's Elytra
