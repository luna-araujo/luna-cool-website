Sugar Bingo is a charming video bingo game developed by Caleta Gaming. It transforms a sweet and imaginative candy shop into a delightful adventure, featuring exclusive mechanics like Lucky Picks for extra rewards. This Caleta release stands out with a new exclusive feature that enhances the thrill of winning: Lucky Picks, offering extra rewards when chosen numbers are drawn. Colorful, playful, and engaging, Sugar Bingo is a captivating title that keeps players entertained and eager for more.

## Game Details

- **Release Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Type**: Video Bingo
- **RTP**: 91.49% - 93.79%
- **Volatility**: High
- **Max Win**: 5120x
- **Tags**: #CANDY, #SWEET, #SUGAR, #FUNNY, #ROBOT

## Features

- EXTRA BALLS
- LUCKY PICKS
