Froggo the Frog is a 3D platformer game featuring tongue-swinging action similar to Spider-Man. Compete against players around the world by submitting your highest score after beating all three levels.

## Game Features

- **Tongue-swinging Action**: Use your tongue to swing like Spider-Man
- **Speedrunning Elements**: Race against the clock
- **Collectables**: Gather flies and pass through hoops
- **Leaderboard System**: Compete with players worldwide

## Gameplay

- Get to the jukebox as fast as possible
- Use W, A, S, D to move and SPACEBAR to jump
- Hold left click to use your tongue and swing
- Pass through hoops and collect flies
- Beat all three levels to submit your score

Made for the olc::BeatTheBoredom game jam.
